About Me

Having studied and worked as a researcher in environmental applications of remote sensing across multiple countries and continents (Italy, USA, Peru, Turkey, Portugal, Vietnam, and the Gambia), I have witnessed firsthand the profound connection between social injustice and land degradation.
For this reason, I am passionate about exploring the intersections of geospatial analysis with a commitment to critical reflection, continuously striving to contribute to meaningful change.
