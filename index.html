Summary

With research and work experience in Remote Sensing for Environmental Applications across Italy, the USA, Peru, Turkey, Portugal, Vietnam, and the Gambia, I've seen firsthand the deep link between social injustice and environmental degradation.
For this reason, I am passionate about harnessing geospatial analysis to deepen our understanding of this connection, striving to drive meaningful change.
